I am an artist and an animator. My work usually follows different experimentations with the intent on mastering either a specific style or effect.

My first body of work is in sculpture where I explore different ways to depict the slow march of time with a defeatist's atitude.

My second body of work is in animation. With animation I typically focus on trying to get the objects in scene to react to one another via dynamics or expressions
